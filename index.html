Rombus

In the year 3070, pirates of space are legion. They go from planet to planet and plunder to recover the last source of wealth in the universe: nuclear ores, which are found only in the heart of some planets.
You are one of those pirates, a member of the Globus crew.
Your mission : recover a maximum of precious stones. But beware, a mining planet is very unstable, so hurry up!
