Rombus is a puzzle game that takes the form of a grid of 8 squares out of 8 containing stones of different colors. The goal of the game is to group them by color in groups of 3 or more, horizontally or vertically, inverting two adjacent gems each time. The goal of the game is to collect them by color in groups of 3 or more, horizontally or vertically, by inverting two adjacent gems each time.

When aligned, they disappear, and those above fall, showing new gems. You will gain bonus points if you manage to align more than 3 identical stones.

When falling, it is possible that gems of the same color will line up, causing a chain reaction, which will also give you bonus points. The more chains there are, the more points you will earn.

At the end of a certain number of chains, you will be able to see appear on your grid a bonus object, a bomb which will allow you to destroy all the gems around.

You have 3 minutes to reach the required number of points at each level.

You will have lost the game if you fail to complete the level within the time allowed, or if no more combination is possible on your grid.

Need help ? If you are stuck at a level, don't panic, a clue will be offered to you after a certain period of inactivity.
